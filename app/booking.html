2. Our Services

Hairlux provides professional beauty, grooming, and wellness services both at our physical locations and through mobile services delivered to your home, office, or hotel within Nigeria. All services are subject to professional availability, location accessibility, and scheduling confirmation.

3. Account Registration

To access certain features, you may be required to create an account and provide accurate, current, and complete information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and all activities carried out under your account; Hairlux is not liable for unauthorized access resulting from a failure to secure your account.

4. Booking Confirmation

All appointments must be made through official booking channels and are only confirmed after payment is successfully received. A confirmation message or email will be sent upon successful booking, and clients are responsible for reviewing details for accuracy.

5. Payment Policy

Full payment is required upfront to secure all bookings, though part-payment may be accepted in special cases provided the balance is completed before service begins. Payments are processed securely via Paystack and Moniepoint using debit cards, bank transfers, or USSD. All prices are listed in Nigerian Naira (NGN) and may be updated without prior notice.

6. Location Policy

Clients must provide a clear, accurate, and accessible location at the time of booking. Any location changes must be communicated before the appointment time, as changes are not permitted once a technician is en route or has arrived.

7A. Health Disclosure Requirement

Clients are required to disclose relevant health conditions, including skin conditions, allergies, and medical sensitivities, prior to receiving services. Hairlux bears no responsibility for outcomes resulting from undisclosed conditions and reserves the right to decline or modify services to mitigate risks.

7B. Minor Policy (Clients Under 18)

Services are not provided to individuals under 18 without explicit consent from a parent or legal guardian. Consent must be provided prior to service, and the parent or guardian may be required to be present. Clients must also ensure availability of water, a clean and safe environment, and minimal interruptions. Any additional services or changes requested during the session must be paid for immediately.

8. Delay & Waiting Policy

A 10-minute grace period is allowed for appointments. After this period, a waiting charge of 5,000 NGN per 15 minutes will apply.

9. Cancellations, Rescheduling & Refunds

A full refund applies only if cancellation is made at least 24 hours before the appointment; cancellations within 24 hours attract a 20% deduction, and cancellations after a technician arrives attract a 50% charge. "No-shows" are charged 50% of the booking value. Rescheduling is permitted with reasonable notice, subject to Hairlux approval based on availability.

10. Safety & Security

The safety of personnel is a top priority, and Hairlux reserves the right to decline or withdraw service without refund if the environment is deemed unsafe, hostile, or presents a security risk.

11. Client Conduct

Clients are expected to maintain professionalism and respect; Hairlux reserves the right to refuse or terminate service and restrict future bookings in cases of abusive language, harassment, or threatening behavior.
