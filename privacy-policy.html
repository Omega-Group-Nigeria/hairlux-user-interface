Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you register or book a service. We also collect usage data automatically, such as device type, IP address, and pages visited, to help improve our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and confirm bookings
  • Deliver services to your location
  • Send booking updates and service notifications
  • Improve the Hairlux platform and customer experience
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

3. Payment Information

All payment processing is handled by Paystack. Hairlux does not store your card details. Please refer to Paystack's Privacy Policy for details on how your payment data is handled.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with trusted service partners (such as payment processors) strictly as necessary to deliver our services. We may also disclose data when required by law or a valid legal process.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data held by Hairlux. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at omegaresourcemgnt@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the platform may not function as intended.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at omegaresourcemgnt@gmail.com or call +234 808 714 1501.
